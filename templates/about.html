Built for Developers, Analysts & Cricket Fans

Powerful APIs designed for analytics, dashboards, visualizations, and cricket applications.

Match Analytics

Venue Insights

API Playground

Structured IPL Data

Cleaned and organized IPL datasets designed for fast querying and developer usage.

Why This Platform Exists

Raw cricket datasets are often difficult to query and integrate into applications. IPL API Platform transforms complex IPL match data into clean, accessible, developer-friendly APIs.

The platform is designed for students, developers, analysts, researchers, and cricket enthusiasts who want reliable IPL data without dealing with raw datasets manually.
